Exploring Tweets

Enter a search term below to find related tweets!

Exploratory Drill down Lateral Everything

Exploratory

This will search for all tweets containing hypernyms of your search term. If you search for "orange," "fruit" is a hypernym of orange.

Lateral

This will search for all tweets containing synonyms of your search term.

Drill Down

This will search for all tweets containing hyponyms of your search term. If you search for "fruit," "orange" is a hyponym of fruit.

Everything

This will combine all three options listed above! Only for the truly adventurous data explorers!
